UoB - Year II notes

Here you will find the most up to date PDF files for the notes. They are summaries of lectures given at UoB. They are intended to aid revision. Please bare in mind the notes are updated hourly but these PDF files are not. They may be up to a few days old. I will aim to keep them as recent as possible.

These notes are not perfect, there will be errors. Please help us by pointing them out! You can find the source code, and you can report errors (under 'Issues') at this webpage: http://github.com/mindfuzz/notes. It is also possible to email us and we will correct errors.
